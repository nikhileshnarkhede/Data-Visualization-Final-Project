Remuneration Statistics by Experience Level

This complex parallel plot gives an overview of data industry salaries at different levels of experience, from entry-level to executive positions. Each colorful line shows the trajectory of an individual's salary over time, crossing over two axes: work year and salary in USD.The first immediate visual clue as to common salary ranges in each experience category comes from density and distribution. Noticeably, executive positions tend to keep towards the top scale of salaries, reflecting the strong value of high-ranking experience and management. This plot underlines not only the spread of salaries in the data sector but also can show career growth-a person might go from entry-level through more senior positions over the years. Such a visualization can be a powerful tool for job seekers, HR professionals, and policy makers all at once, giving a very important at-one-glance comparison of possible earnings across the spectrum of industries.
